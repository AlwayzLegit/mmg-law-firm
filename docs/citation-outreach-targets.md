# Citation & Link-Building Target List

> Grounded in a Semrush referring-domain gap analysis (June 2026) between
> `mmg-lawfirm.com` and the top local PI/employment competitors
> (`agemianlawgroup.com` AS 27 / 661 ref domains, `boyamianlaw.com` AS 9 / 152).
> **MMG is currently AS 7 / ~93 ref domains — and ~90 of those are
> auto-generated scraper junk.** Only two legitimate citations exist today:
> Super Lawyers (AS 60) and Expertise.com (AS 42). This list closes that gap.

## How to use this

For **every** listing, use the firm's NAP **character-for-character identically**.
Inconsistent name/address/phone is the #1 reason citations fail to move local rank.

```
Name:    MMG Law Firm
Address: 100 W Broadway, Suite 540, Glendale, CA 91210
Phone:   (818) 539-7969
Email:   info@mmg-lawfirm.com
Website: https://mmg-lawfirm.com
Hours:   Mon–Fri 9:00 AM–6:00 PM
```

Category everywhere: **Personal Injury Attorney** (primary) + **Employment
Attorney** (secondary where a second category is allowed). Languages:
**English, Armenian, Russian** — never "Spanish."

Work the list **top to bottom**. The ordering is by authority-per-effort:
the highest-AS, lowest-friction wins are first.

## Housekeeping — toxic/junk links

The ~90 scraper domains pointing at `mmg-lawfirm.com` (the `118.139.x.x`
Singapore cluster: `linksnatcher.*`, `websiterace.com`, `getwebsiteworth.com`,
`allwebsitesdirectory.com`, etc.) are auto-generated and worthless. They're
**not** an emergency — Google generally ignores them — but if Search Console
ever flags manual-action risk, compile them into a `disavow.txt` and submit via
the GSC Disavow Tool. **Do not disavow Tier 0–4 targets.** Leave this until
after the real citations are built; it's the lowest-priority item here.

## Suggested cadence

- **Week 1:** Tier 0 (#1–4) + the two highest-AS legal fixes (#5 Justia link, #6 BBB, #7 Avvo).
- **Week 2:** Rest of Tier 1 (#8–17).
- **Week 3:** Tier 2 (#18–22).
- **Week 4 + ongoing:** Tier 3 local/community (#23–27) and Tier 4 editorial (#28–31) — these never really "finish."

Track each in a simple sheet: *Target · URL · Status · Date · Live link*. After
~4–6 weeks of consistent submissions, re-run the Semrush referring-domain report
to confirm the new citations are indexed and the Authority Score is climbing.
